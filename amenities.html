Amenities

Where to Stay

While we do recommend staying directly at Edgefield. It tends to book up early due to concerts and events. In case you can't get a reservation, or would like your own bathroom and modern amenities (Edgefield has European-style bathrooms) we recommend staying at one of the following hotels:

  • Best Western Plus (~0.7 miles)
  • Travelodge (~1 mile)
  • Holiday Inn Express (~1.3 miles)
  • Comfort Inn (~1.3 miles)

What to Do

Edgefield has a wealth of things to do. From golfing, to wine tasting, nightly movies or multiple restaurants. It's a great weekend getaway.

We recommend:

  • Setting up a tee time in advance for the par 3, 20 hole golf course (your clubs can be rented)
  • Check out the nighly movies upon your arrival. Edgefield shows current movies for a few dollars every night.
  • Be sure to check out the different restaurants in the compound. We highly recommend the Cajun Tater tots
  • If you're staying at Edgefield. The spa with walk up bar is open until 10pm. Bring your swimsuit.
  • Walk through the grounds and check out the glass blowing, gift shops, gardens and small shops. Drinks are available all over.
  • If you're planning on staying the weekend, Multnomah Falls and the Columbia River are both within driving distance.

Wedding Location

Encompassing a 74-acre parcel of farmland at the mouth of the spectacular Columbia River Gorge Scenic Area, Edgefield is a 20-minute drive to or from the center of downtown Portland and about 15 minutes from Portland International Airport.
